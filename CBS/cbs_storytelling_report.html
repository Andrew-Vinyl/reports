The Engagement Challenge

While we are driving more of the right traffic to the site, the data shows we need to work harder to keep them there.

The GA4 Reality Check

  • Bounce Rates are Climbing: Overall bounce jumped from 64.9% to 74.5%. This trend is visible across both Organic Search (72.89%) and Paid Search (39.61%).
  • Session Duration is Dropping: Visitors are spending less time exploring the site, with average duration down from ~2 minutes to just 1m 20s.

The gap between Traffic and Lead Generation is where we lose users.

From Traffic to Traction: Next Steps

1. Fix the Funnel (Lead Gen)

We need to plug the leak. Launch specific lead magnets (assessments, playbooks, templates) immediately. Ensure there are clear, compelling CTAs across all blogs and videos, and track form fills tied directly to MOFU content.

2. Improve Content Stickiness

To combat the rising bounce rate, update the blog format to incorporate "in-article ads" pointing to other relevant CBS content. Add "case briefs with proof of outcomes" to build trust faster during the reading experience.

3. Optimize Hub Pages

Get necessary approvals and fill in the CBS additional content requirements for our main keyword hub pages. These pages must answer the user's intent so thoroughly that they have no reason to bounce back to Google.

4. Active Social Push

Continue manually inviting targeted prospects to follow the LinkedIn page. Test actively commenting with links or sharing LinkedIn Articles to drive engaged, high-intent traffic directly from social channels.

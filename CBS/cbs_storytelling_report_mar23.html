From Traffic to Traction: Next Steps

1. Cancel RankMath & Consolidate SEO Stack

Vinyl will reach out to Phil to cancel RankMath. Since the site is already ranking for several key terms, we are moving forward with Yoast, which now provides the functionality we originally needed — without disrupting the existing SEO foundation.

2. Build Out Spoke Pages

Re-purpose high-performing blog content as "spoke" pages anchored to our three priority hub topics: Operational Excellence, Private Equity Consulting, and Continuous Improvement. This deepens topical authority and improves internal linking structure.

3. Lead Generation Planning

Scott, Ed and Keith have aligned on next steps. Scott will present a formal lead generation plan at the 3/23 meeting to establish clear MOFU pathways and conversion targets.

4. LinkedIn Engagement Optimization

Continue posting and re-sharing 3x per week. Invite followers on behalf of CBS and review data from commenting-with-link and LinkedIn Article experiments to determine what drives the most high-intent traffic.

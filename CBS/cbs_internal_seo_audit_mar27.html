Executive Summary

Where CBS Stands

Section 6

AEO Readiness (Answer Engine Optimization)

D+ →
This is our weakest area and the one most likely to draw attention from TalentLink. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) readiness requires structured content, comprehensive schema markup, and authority signals that AI engines can parse. We have basic Yoast schema (Organization, Website, Breadcrumb, Article) but almost nothing beyond that. We've identified this gap internally and rated our own schema implementation at 4/10. FAQ schema is now registering on some pages, but the broader AEO strategy — content structured for AI consumption, entity-based optimization, comprehensive schema types — is nascent.

Current Schema Implementation

  • Basic Yoast defaults: Organization, Website, Breadcrumb, Article
  • FAQ schema: Recently implemented on pages with FAQ blocks (just beginning to register)
  • Missing: No FAQPage schema, Service schema, HowTo schema, or custom JSON-LD beyond Yoast defaults
  • Content structure: Not optimized for AI answer extraction (no clear Q&A format, no concise definitions)

AEO Readiness Checklist

Service Schema Not implemented High
Enhanced Organization Schema Basic only High
LocalBusiness Schema Not implemented Medium
Article Schema on Blogs Yoast default Medium
Breadcrumb Schema Implemented Low

Entity optimization Minimal High

What Needs to Happen

  • Add FAQ sections to all 5 service pages with FAQPage schema markup
  • Implement Service schema for each consulting service line
  • Enhance Organization schema with ConsultingBusiness type, industries served, social profiles, leadership
  • Add LocalBusiness schema if applicable to market positioning
  • Verify Article schema on all blog posts and optimize for AI extraction
  • Restructure content with clear definitions, Q&A sections, concise answer paragraphs for AI parsing
  • Entity optimization: Ensure CBS is consistently described as an entity across all structured data
Self-Assessment: 4/10 on Schema Implementation

We have the foundation (Yoast basics) but are missing the advanced structures that make content AI-friendly. This is one of our clearest "we know what to do" gaps — the roadmap is clear, execution is what's needed.
